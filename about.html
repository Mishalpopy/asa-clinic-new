About Us

ASA Clinic

Imtibish is a pioneering group of visionaries promoting authentic, natural health systems deeply embodied in tradition within the spirit of nature. We’re building a host of institutions such as wellness centers, hospitals & clinics, educational & training centers, research centers and modern manufacturing units for herbal medicines. This is augmented by initiatives such as periodical & academic publications, health-clubs and digital channels. Besides keeping people healthy, imtibish also believes in the importance of an ethical and moral lifestyle as an essential complement to good health. Founded in 2015, imtibish is the first of its kind institute in the world that offers the entire spectrum of Unani treatments in all its dimensions, under one roof. The core philosophy of the institute is to guard the systems of Unani, Ayurveda and other systems of natural medicine with integrity, ethics and discipline.
